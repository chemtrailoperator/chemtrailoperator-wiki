# Endgame Alpha Main Synopsis
# THIS DOCUMENT IS INCOMPLETE AND DOES NOT REFLECT MAJOR CHANGES IN V0.5 ONWARDS

Heading names refer to the file in which the missions are located, StoryMissions-<sequence>-<name>

### Prologue

1. Any Free Worlds planet
    Upon landing, you see who you think is Raven in the spaceport. Upon following her, she disappears.

2. Any Deep planet
    Upon landing, you see Raven again, but when you follow her, she disappears again.

3. Any Syndicate planet
    Upon landing, you follow Raven again, and she startles you, telling you to go to Geminus in the Castor system to receive instructions.

4. Geminus
    Upon landing on Geminus, you receive a message from Raven saying she'll be in touch, with a payment.

### Espionage

5. Any Human planet, 30 days after last Raven contact
    A group of terrorists bombs a remembrance day celebration on Earth.

6. Any Human planet, 90 days after last Raven contact (not dependent on bombing)
    Raven contacts you, requesting you to report to Navy Intelligence on Geminus to receive instructions.

7. Geminus
    Raven tells you that the terrorists that bombed Earth were not normal terrorists, and that they have odd ties. She asks you to meet up with a Navy Intelligence operative on Mutiny in the Gienah system to investigate.

8. Mutiny
   You eventually make contact with the operative, and he requests escort to Greenrock in the Shaula system.

9. Greenrock
    When you land at Greenrock, the operative tells you to meet him in the spaceport after he makes some inquiries.

10. Greenrock Spaceport
    After waiting for the operative for hours and being told to leave by a bouncer, you are given the choice to either fight the bouncer or go in search of the operative. If you fight the bouncer, you die.

    If you decide to look for the operative, his last known coordinates lead you to a truck parked on top of the hill. The operative is inside the truck, which is filled with explosives. If you try to cut the operative loose, you die.
 
    After his death, the operative's name is revealed to be Gabriel Danforth just as a massive pirate fleet forces you to take off and flee to Geminus. The pirate fleet turns out to be an Alpha fleet.

11. Geminus
    On Geminus, you are requested to carry Raven to the Oathkeepers to plan a response to the Alpha presence.

12. Farpoint
    As you plan with Admiral Danforth and Raven, an Alpha fleet occupies Greenrock, and you are dispatched to destroy the fleet as the Navy cannot receive authorization to enter the pirate system through the Free Worlds in time.



14. New Iceland
    On New Iceland, you receive a commission as a commander in the Republic Navy and are ordered to report to Earth for official confirmation

15. Earth
    On Earth, you are confirmed as a commander and receive access to patrol jobs, the ability to purchase Gunships, Dropships, and Frigates,and the right to wear the Navy Uniform, which can be bought at large Navy or population centers.


16. Any human planet, 97 days after commissioning into the Navy
    You are ordered to report to Geminus for special service.

17. Geminus
    You are ordered to retrieve a classified data chip on loan to the Free Worlds from Solace in the Pherkad system

18. Pherkad
    You are ambushed by a massive Alpha fleet, and instead of completing the pickup you must return to Geminus
    The Southern and CCOR pirate worlds beyond Pherkad are now displayed as Alpha territory

19. Geminus
    Upon landing on Geminus, you receive orders to travel back to New Iceland.
